Why these offers are designed this way

The Hormozi Value Equation
⬆ Dream Outcome
"The result they actually want"
⬆ Perceived Likelihood
"Proof it will work FOR THEM"
⬇ Time Delay
"How fast they get their first win"
⬇ Effort & Sacrifice
"SOPs, templates, done-with-you"
Value = (Dream Outcome × Likelihood) ÷ (Time Delay × Effort)

"If effort and time go to zero, value becomes infinite. That's what we're building."

Why 3 profiles and not 1?

The three ICPs are not separate audiences — they are the same person at different stages of their journey. Diana becomes Ron. Ron becomes Marco. Nuno's role is to serve each stage with the right offer, so every interaction moves them one level up. The webinar is the entry point for all three.
